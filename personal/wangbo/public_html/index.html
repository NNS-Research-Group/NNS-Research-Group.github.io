About Me

I am a 3rd year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Fengyuan Ren. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in computer science and technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2014. My current research interest in HTTP adaptive video streaming.
