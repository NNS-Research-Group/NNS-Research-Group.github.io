About Me

I am a 5th year Ph.D. student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Fengyuan Ren. Before that, I received the B.E. degree in computer science and technology, and the Second B.E. degree in mathematics and applied mathematics from Tsinghua University in 2014. My research interests include data center networking and congestion control, network performance modeling and analysis, and high performance stack. My CV is available in PDF format here (last updated on April 2017).
